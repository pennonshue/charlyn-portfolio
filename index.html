Hi, I'm Charlyn

I started doodling in elementary school while my grandma took me to Saturday art classes for fun in Shanghai. Overtime, that turned into oil painting, linocutting, and other forms of art to keep my hands busy when there's a movie, music, or podcast playing the background. I enjoy filling my living space with art, giving it to friends and family, and hope to keep on doing so!
